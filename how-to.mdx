<Callout type="success">
  <b>Quick and easy!</b> Follow these simple steps to get your new plugin up and running in Mautic.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Buy from our website">
    Visit <a href="https://mtcextendee.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><b>mtcextendee.com</b></a> and choose your plugin.<br />
    All plugins are from trusted Mautic community developers.<br />
    Our support team is here to help, usually within 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your download link and license key">
    After purchase, youâ€™ll receive a download link and license key by email.<br />
    <Callout type="info">
      <b>Tip:</b> The download link always gives you access to the latest version of your plugin, forever.
    </Callout>
    Download the plugin and keep your license key safe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the plugin">
    Unzip the downloaded file (if needed) and copy the plugin folder to your Mautic <code>plugins/</code> directory.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear the cache">
    In your Mautic root folder, run:<br />
    <code>php bin/console cache:clear</code>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reload the plugin list">
    Run:<br />
    <code>php bin/console mautic:plugins:reload</code>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Regenerate Mautic assets">
    Run:<br />
    <code>php bin/console mautic:assets:generate</code>
  </Step>
  <Step title="All done!">
    Your plugin is now installed and ready to use.<br />
    Enjoy the new features!
  </Step>
</Steps>
