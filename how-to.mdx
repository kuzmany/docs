## Plugins Store for Mautic â€“ How it works?

A simple guide to buy, install, or upgrade your Mautic Extendee plugins.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Buy app directly from website">
    All packages are from trusted Mautic community developers.<br />
    We provide the best support within 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive download link, license key and install manually">
    You will get a download link and license key after purchase.<br />
    Install the package manually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the plugin to plugins/YourBundleName">
    Just copy the plugin folder to your Mautic <code>plugins/</code> directory.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Run:<br />
    <code>php bin/console cache:clear</code>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh plugin list">
    Run:<br />
    <code>php bin/console mautic:plugins:reload</code>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh Mautic assets">
    Run:<br />
    <code>php bin/console mautic:assets:generate</code>
  </Step>
  <Step title="It's done">
    Enjoy your new plugin!
  </Step>
</Steps>
