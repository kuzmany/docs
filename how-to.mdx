---
title: How to Buy and Install Plugins
description: Step-by-step guide for buying, installing, and upgrading Mautic Extendee plugins.
sidebarTitle: How to
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Buy app directly from website">
    Buy from our <a href="https://mtcextendee.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">mtcextendee.com</a>.<br />
    All plugins are from trusted Mautic community developers.<br />
    We provide fast support, usually within 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive download link, license key, and install manually">
    After purchase, you will get a download link and license key.<br />
    Install the package manually.<br />
    <Callout type="info">
      <b>Note:</b> The download link always gives you access to the latest version of the plugin, forever.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the plugin to plugins/YourBundleName">
    Copy the plugin folder to your Mautic <code>plugins/</code> directory.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Run:<br />
    <code>php bin/console cache:clear</code>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh plugin list">
    Run:<br />
    <code>php bin/console mautic:plugins:reload</code>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Regenerate Mautic assets">
    Run:<br />
    <code>php bin/console mautic:assets:generate</code>
  </Step>
  <Step title="It's done">
    Your plugin is ready to use!
  </Step>
</Steps>
