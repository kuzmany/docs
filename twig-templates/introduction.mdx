Want to make your Mautic messages feel truly personal? The **Twig Templates plugin** is here to help.

With this plugin, you can:
*   **Add smart logic:** Show different content based on contact details, dates, or other conditions.
*   **Insert dynamic content:** Pull in data from external sources, RSS feeds, or complex contact fields.
*   **Use it everywhere:** Enhance emails, landing pages, and even text messages.
*   **Go beyond basic tokens:** Move past simple `{contactfield=...}` replacements and create unique, tailored experiences using easy-to-learn Twig code.

<Note>
  **Compatibility:** Works with Mautic 4, Mautic 5, and Mautic 6.
</Note>
