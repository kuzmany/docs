Make your Mautic messages much more personal with the Twig Templates plugin. It lets you add smart logic and custom content right into your emails, landing pages, and text messages using simple Twig code. This means you can go way beyond basic `{contactfield=firstname}` tokens and create unique experiences for everyone.

**Compatibility:** Mautic 4, Mautic 5, and Mautic 6
