Unlock hyper-personalization in Mautic with the **Twig Templates plugin**! Go beyond standard tokens and craft truly unique experiences for your audience.

**Why use Twig Templates?**

*   **Smarter Communication:** Display content dynamically based on contact data, dates, segments, or even external information.
*   **Rich Content Integration:** Seamlessly embed data from RSS feeds, external URLs, or complex JSON stored in contact fields.
*   **Unified Personalization:** Apply advanced logic consistently across emails, landing pages, and text messages.
*   **Reusable Logic:** Create complex snippets once and reuse them easily across multiple assets.
*   **Developer-Friendly:** Leverage the power and flexibility of the standard Twig templating engine.

<Note>
  **Compatibility:** Fully compatible with Mautic 4, Mautic 5, and Mautic 6.
</Note>
