# ✨ Twig Templates Plugin

Welcome to the **Twig Templates** plugin for Mautic!  
This powerful extension lets you create reusable content blocks, personalize your marketing, and supercharge your emails, landing pages, and more.

<Note>
  <b>Compatibility:</b> Fully compatible with Mautic 4, Mautic 5, and Mautic 6.
</Note>

---

## 🚀 Why Use Twig Templates?

- **Save time:** Reuse content and logic across multiple emails and pages.
- **Personalize at scale:** Use dynamic tokens and Twig logic for advanced personalization.
- **Stay organized:** Manage your templates in one place and keep your content consistent.
- **Integrate rich data:** Embed content from RSS feeds, external URLs, or JSON fields.
- **Developer-friendly:** Leverage the full power of the Twig templating engine.

---

## 🧩 What Can You Do?

- **Create reusable snippets** for greetings, signatures, product blocks, and more.
- **Embed templates** inside other templates for modular content.
- **Leverage Twig syntax** for conditions, loops, and dynamic data.
- **Access Mautic tokens** like `contact`, `company`, and custom objects.
