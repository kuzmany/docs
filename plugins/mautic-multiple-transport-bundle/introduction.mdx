<note>
  <b>Works with Mautic 4, 5, and 6.</b>
</note>

Manage and use multiple email transports in Mautic with just a few clicks.

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="Assign per Email" icon="mail">
    Choose a different transport for each email you send.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Assign per User" icon="user">
    Set a custom transport for each user (contact owner).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use Owner's Transport" icon="users">
    Let emails use the contact owner's transport automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Easy Setup" icon="settings">
    Add, edit, and test unlimited transports (SMTP, Amazon SES, Mailjet, Sendgrid, and more).
  </Card>
  <Card title="API Ready" icon="code">
    Automate everything with a simple API.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

**Requirements**
- Mautic 4, 5, or 6
- PHP 8.0 or higher (recommended)
- At least one working email transport
