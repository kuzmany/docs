---
description: Frequently asked questions about the Multiple Transport Plugin.
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How can I send emails with the contact owner's transport?">
    Assign a transport to the user (owner) in their profile. Then, in the email settings, turn on "Use Owner Custom Transport".
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use a different transport other than SMTP?">
    Yes, you can use any transport supported by Mautic, like SMTP, Amazon SES, Mailjet, Sendgrid, and more.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I test if a transport works?">
    Edit the transport and click the "Test" button. A test email will be sent.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if no transport is set for an email or user?">
    Mautic will use the default transport from your main configuration.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is this plugin compatible with all Mautic versions?">
    Yes, it works with Mautic 4 (SMTP only), and Mautic 5/6 (all transports).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
